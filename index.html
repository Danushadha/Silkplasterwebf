What is Silk Plaster

SILK PLASTER is a fabric texture basis liquid wall covering meterial , which is intentionally design for a luxurious feeling to an elegant perspective at your own premises .

Which origin at Russia in 1972 , but among the growth of most fascinated people it has patnated in many countries over the world wide today.

SILK PLASTER comes as a raw material in a packet weigh at 1kg, which has to be mixed with water at a certain level and should be kept for 12 hours to get fully soaked and ready to apply.

At the context SILK PLASTER conforms that it is totally eco-freindly product. Also certfied by european authorities.

Application Instruction

Before applying silk plaster make sure that surface must be dry , clean of an even white colour without any stains. Remove old wallpaper, lime scale metal and wooden objects etc. and their derivatives.

Apply any suitable, preferable white water proof based primer or original "silk plaster primer" in two layers with recomended drying time between layers 3 - 4 hours.

Step:- 1
Pour room temperature water into plastic container of volume not less than 12 leteres based on below calculation
Fort Art, East, Nord, Provence Master, Optima, Ecodecor, Standard, Prestige, Victoria, Airline, West, South Relief, Premium
2.5 ltrs per 1 packet 4.5 ltrs per 1 packet 6.0 ltrs per 1 packet 7.0 ltrs per 1 packet
Step:- 2
If required add the package of glitter, mineral and/or decorative addives firstly into water and stir it.

Step:- 3
shake the content of the bag of wall coating to ensure even distribution of the mixture through the packet. Pour the mixture into the plastic tub and mix them well by the hands, straining through the fingers to avoid formation of glue clots.

Step:- 4
Leave the prepared batch for recomended period (12 hours) in order to get properly soaked befor applying. for convenient storage make use of the original packaging bag. Once you ready to start applying, mix the soaked packs at once into thee plastic tub and again mixed the soaked batch by hands well.
Step:- 5
Taking small portions, apply the soaked material with strokes of plastic trowel evenly on the surface at a thickness of 1 - 2 mm. for an ideal application. After you'e finished, and within 1 - 2 hours , using slide lighting , smooth out the surface with the plastic trowel, soaking it in water from time to time.

Note :-

Do not directly contact water after the material dried on the surface

Becuse of above we don't recomend Silk Plaster for other than interior decorations

Silk Plaster can be use on any surface where you apply the primer (ceeling , boards etc )

If you wish to change the applied texture later , you can re-apply on the same surface

We gurantee Silk Plaster will be last for 10 years if you mix the correct water levels when you're preparing mixture
